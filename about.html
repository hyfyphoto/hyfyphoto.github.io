ABOUT ME

My name is Mahir Sufian and I am currently a senior at Rutgers University and will be attending medical school next year at New Jersey Medical School.
Photography is my biggest passion: it is my hobby, my stress relief, my art, and my work. While I enjoy exploring the world with and through my camera lens, I want to share that experience with you. I am experienced in event photography, portraits, and landscapes. I also have extensive experience in post processing techniques with Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop. Most importantly, I want you to be as excited by the results as I am by the process. So allow me to share my creative expression with you and help you make your vision a reality.
