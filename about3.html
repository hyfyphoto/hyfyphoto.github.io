ABOUT ME

My name is Mahir Sufian and I am currently a senior at Rutgers University and will be attending medical school next year at New Jersey Medical School.

Photography is my greatest passion: it is my hobby, my stress relief, my art, and my work. While I enjoy exploring the world with and through my camera lens, I want to share that experience with you. I am experienced in event photography, portraits, and landscapes. I also have extensive experience in post processing techniques with Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop.

Most importantly, my desire is to see that my clients are as excited about their results as I am about the process. Allow me to share my creative expression with you to make your visions a reality.
